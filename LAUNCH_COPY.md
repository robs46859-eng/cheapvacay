## Beta Launch Post

I built CheapVacay India to solve a simple problem: most trip tools help you browse destinations, but they do not help you understand what the whole trip will cost before you commit.

The current beta lets you:

- compare practical transport choices
- estimate total trip cost
- save draft trip plans
- ask an AI assistant for budget-focused guidance

Right now I am looking for early users planning real India trips so I can learn where estimates feel useful and where they need to improve.

## Feedback Prompt
